Research

My research focuses on Graph Data stream mining. I work on developing algorithms for efficient graph stream processing which has application like Influence Maximization for viral marketing in social data stream. Currently I am working on supporting distributed graph stream processing on in-memory distributed graph processing systems like GraphX or Giraph.
